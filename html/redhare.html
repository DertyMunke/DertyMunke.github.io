Red Hare Games


This was the first big project that my company, Random Sin Productions took on in 2013. A friend of ours owns a brewery, Red Hare Brewing Company, in Marietta, GA, and we met with them and designed the concept. Soon after, we began developement and had a first release in the beginning of 2016 on the iTunes and Google Play stores. Currently, we are still fixing bugs and making some changes to improve playability, according to feedback that we have recieved.

Game Play


You are a bartender at a Red Hare Bar and there are three regular customers, a red hare, a sexy fox, and a working class bear, that cannot get enough Red Hare brew. If you impress them with your bartending skills, you will earn reputation and get big time tips. With enough reputation, each customer will challenge you to a different game where you can wager your tips to increase your cash flow. Keep scrolling to learn more about the different games!

Future Plans


We are getting rid of the reputation and implementing a gradual, level by level introduction to each customer. Instead of unlocking the customers with experience and earning a chance to play their mini-games, you will be able to play the beer toss level to earn cash and you can use that cash anytime to play any one of the mini-games, whenever and whichever you choose.

Mini-Games


Beer Toss


A shuffleboard inspired game with a count down timer, where you will take the customers order, pour the beer, and slide it down the bar to them. If you pour the correct beer and land it next to the customer you will get bonus time and the maximum tip amount to add to your bank roll.

Beer Pong


Your traditional style beer pong game, with a re-rack option and you can even slap the ball away after it contacts any other object in the game, including the cup.

Flip Cup


This challenging game of flip cup simulates drinking each beer, placing in back on the table, then flipping the cup on its top with a swyping motion. You can reset the cup by swyping downward, so you do not have to wait for the cup to land if you know it will be a bad flip.

Punching Game


A remake of the punching bag strength game that you might find in the corner of a dive bar. We have included an animated strength meter and the punching bag is a bee hive, which is why it is the bear customer's favorite game.
