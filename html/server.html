Server Pro


Server Pro is an app to help professionals in the service industry keep track of their tips, tipouts, and more. We started working on this project at Random Sin Productions a little over a year ago, when we were nearing the end of our first project, Red Hare Games. Server Pro is currently on Google Play and iOS, in beta testing. We designed it in Unity3D using C#, and we are finalizing the app features now by adding an Amazon AWS instance with a MySql database, used to backup the users input data. I am using Eclipse to design the backend with Java and Java Servlets, so the app can communicate with the server.

Functionality


Server Pro is an easy way to keep track of your total earnings after tipouts and expenses. You can save multiple profiles for different jobs and look at the year-to-date, monthly, daily, and am or pm shifts for a single job, or combine the profiles for a quick view of your total earnings in any of these time frames, as well as custom time frames. The app provides an easy way to send text confirmation to the receiver of a tipout and records their name and tipped out total for future lookup. Also, after you enter your daily figures, your percentages, tipouts, and totals are all calculated for you and displayed for you to review.

Future Plans


After we have completed the app, we will pitch it to major and local restaurant groups to add their profile data, such as their tipout percentages and who they tipout, so that as an employee you can select a pre-made profile instead of having to manually set it up. We will also try to partner with a major POS system, to potentially integrate the app with the POS in the restaurant and can link weekly schedules and contact information with the employees straight to the app on their phone or desktop.
