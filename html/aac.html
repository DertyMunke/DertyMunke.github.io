AAC Application


AAC is an augmentative and alternative communication app, to help with speech and language development. We, Random Sin Productions have teamed up with a Speech Pathologist at Speech Abilities and have custom made the application according to her specifications. The design and most of the functionality is complete, but production has been halted due to a wait on the contracted artist assets. There are over a thousand images that need to be complete and then organized by the speech pathologist into different levels of functioning patients that will benifit from using the app. Once the assets and functional design have been complete, we will continue production.


Functionality


Teachers and parents will setup the app and give it to their speech patients to help them develop their communication skills. The app comes with standard setting, so it is playable out of the box, yet is fully customizable to fit the child's or patient's development needs.

Future Plans


After completion, our Speech Pathologist, Speech Abilities, will use the app with her patients and teach their parents to use the app in her clinic at Paradise Speech , in Irvine CA. She also has plans to travel to speech conventions worldwide to promote the application, as she feels many children and adults with disabilities can benifit from its function and affordability.
