About my games...


The first game I ever made was when I joined the VGDC (Video Game Development Club) three years ago, during my first week at the University of California, Irvine. I already had a passion for programming, but after the first game I contributed to, I had no doubt that one day I would make a career out of video game development, one way or another. I love the diety level of creativity that goes into game production. The direction that the world turns, all the way to the personality of the creatures on it, are all created by a team of gods, and my god complex is satisfied as a member of that team.
