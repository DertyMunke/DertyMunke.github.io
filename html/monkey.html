Monkey Storm


I teamed up with three other individuals who had never worked with Unity, in an effort to teach them the ins and outs of using that IDE. It took us about three months to complete a working game prototype and I published it on the Google Play store, so we could show off our little project. I was in charge of the overall gameplay, animations, design, and combining all of the members individual tasks into one final project.

Game Play


"You are a monkey named Chacco and you begin by collecting branches to build your home in the jungle. There are evil monkeys that try to destroy you and your home and you have to pick up bananas to use as weapons against these predators. After defeating the evil monkeys, the Acme Tree Company shows up with bulldozers and inadvertently destroys your home. You must outrun them until they get low on fuel, then escape back into the jungle. Now, you must rebuild your home and the cycle starts over with increasing difficulty."

Future Plans


We plan to remake the game as a 3D side scroller, add a story line with animations, and improve on the goals and challenges Chacco faces.

Levels


Level 1


You have found the perfect location to build your home. Swing from branch to branch, locating the perfect tree parts to bring back and build your home. Falling to the ground is your only enemy in this level, so judge your distants correctly, or else... splat.

Level 2


You live comfortably in your home until the evil monkeys find your hideout and attack with reckless abandon. You must navigate through the trees and collect bananas to throw at them until they retreat.

Level 3


The Acme Paper Company is just outside your front door. You hear the bulldozers and must outrun the destrucive path of the dozers, before they over take you and run you through.
