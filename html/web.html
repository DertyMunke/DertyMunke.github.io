About my web...


I began learning about web development about 6 months ago when I took an upper division class at the University of California, Irvine, in which I built a mock ecommerce website, Fabflix ,that sells DVD's. In the class I was exposed to HTML, CSS, JS, JSP's, Servlets, Java, Apache, and Tomcat. Soon after, I began building this website to show the world what I am doing and hopefully help me to find employment. I am currently developing another mock ecommerce website in an effort to improve my skills and to better market myself. In the future I would love to work on games and applications that use the web to bring people together and accomplish digital acheivments.
